User Guide

Welcome to Mandazi Sales Tracker

Your complete guide to managing your mandazi business efficiently. Learn how to track sales, manage inventory, and grow your business.

Recording Sales

Learn how to record each sale quickly and accurately. Track every transaction with ease.

  • Navigate to the main dashboard
  • Select the products and quantities
  • Add customer name (optional)
  • Click "Record Sale" to complete

Managing Inventory

Keep track of your stock levels and know when to restock. Never run out of ingredients!

  • Go to Settings > Inventory
  • Add your ingredients and products
  • Set minimum stock alerts
  • Update quantities after each sale

M-Pesa Payments

Track all your M-Pesa transactions. Monitor payments received and sent.

  • Click on "Payments" in the menu
  • View all M-Pesa transactions
  • Filter by date or transaction type
  • Link payments to sales

Reports & Invoices

Generate detailed reports, invoices, and receipts. Export to PDF with QR codes.

  • Go to "Sales History"
  • Filter by date range
  • Click on any sale to view invoice
  • Download PDF with QR code

Profile Settings

Customize your profile and business settings. Update information anytime.

  • Click on your profile icon
  • Update business information
  • Change profile picture
  • Manage notification preferences

💡 Pro Tips

Daily Summaries

Check your daily sales summary every evening to track your progress and identify popular items.

Low Stock Alerts

Set up alerts for essential ingredients like flour, sugar, and cooking oil to never run out.

Customer Names

Adding customer names helps build relationships and enables better tracking of regulars.

Backup Data

Regularly export your sales data to keep a backup. Use the Reports feature for this.

Consistent Pricing

Keep your prices consistent. Update them in Settings when needed and communicate changes.

Mobile Friendly

Access your dashboard from any device. The app works great on phones for on-the-go tracking.
